A Few Things About Me

I am a meteorologist from KS who is obsessed with all thing weather and science. I used to teach at the college level and am currently offering short classes at our church for younger kids. Our latest endeavor was to create a tornado in a bottle. I am also a mom to an awesome kiddo and two dogs who think they own our home. Both are hound mutts, but our big dog is mixed with a German Shepherd and earns his place in our hearts and home by protecting us from all dogs walking by the house, as well as the mailman and many squirrels.

My intersts are very varied. I love all things that make me laugh out loud, with bonus points to things that make me actually snort. I love talking about my dogs, my husband, and my son, and, not so suprisingly, weather.

Things That Make Me Laugh

There are so many sad things in this world that I really value a good laugh to help keep me centered. Coffee and dog memes as well as any that show the "fun" side of kids.

Dogs

I am a sucker for dogs, no matter how obnoxious they are. We have had many pups over the years, and we are positive that they keep our family running smoothly...just wish they would learn to fold laundry

Family

I could talk endlessly about how amazing my husband is, or how neuotic he can be. I can also spend hours talking about our son, who is both the apple of my eye, and the child that will drive me to drink.

Favorite Foods

I LOVE food. I am an emotional eater. There is food for happy days, food for sad days, food for boring days...I just love food! I love to cook and bake, but I especially love experimenting with traditional foods!

Weather

I spent 10 years working on my undergraduate degree in Atmospheric Science. I have wanted to learn more about weather since I was a young kiddo. I was terrified of storms, and someone told me that the more you learn about them, the less scary they are.
